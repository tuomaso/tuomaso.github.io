Two papers accepted to ICLR 2023! [CLIP-Dissect](https://arxiv.org/abs/2204.10965)(Spotlight) presents a new efficient and automated way to describe hidden layer neurons in vision models. [Label-Free Concept Bottleneck Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2304.06129) proposes a way to turn existing black-box models into interpretable Concept Bottleneck Models where each neuron corresponds to a human understandable concept.
