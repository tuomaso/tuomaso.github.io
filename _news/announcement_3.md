Our paper [The Importance of Prompt Tuning for Automated Neuron Explanations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.06200) was accepted to NeurIPS 2023 workshop [ATTRIB](https://attrib-workshop.cc/)! This paper shows that simple prompt tuning can significantly increase efficiency and accuracy of automated explanations of neurons in LLMs.
